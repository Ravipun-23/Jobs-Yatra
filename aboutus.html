About us

Jobs Yatra is Nepal's fastest-growing career portal. You may easily search for and apply for jobs on the Jobs Yatra website, and you will enjoy a stress-free application process. At Jobs Yatra, you can look for job openings. Thousands of job openings from top organizations, industries, and locations await you! The innovative features of Jobs Yatra allow you to locate your ideal job in minutes and apply for it with a single click. You can also track your applications and evaluate your CV performance.
